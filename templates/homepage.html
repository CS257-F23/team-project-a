Exoplanet Info Getter

Welcome to the Exoplanet Info Getter! Here are our avaliable features:

  • /planet_info/"Planet Name"
    • Gets info about the chosen planet
  • /check_goldilocks/"Planet Name"
    • Checks if the chosen planet is in the Goldilocks Zone (i.e. potentially habitable!)

Type the name of the planet you'd like to learn about in place of "Planet Name". Use underscores in place of spaces. Capitalization matters.

Example: To get info about the planet "14 Her b", add /planet_info/14_Her_b to the end of the url.
